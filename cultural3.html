COD has been developed since 2003 by many different companies, But Infinity Ward were the original creators of this game. (Franchise)

In December 2013 Call of Duty released their newest series Ghost. Ghost is the thirteenths series of Call of Duty. It all started with their first game called Call of Duty which was a based on World War I, through time they improved the graphic of the game and the story line. It all started with world war I, then the cold war, then went to the future to 2025 and in their latest game Ghost the story is about the near future nuclear destruction of middle east. (Franchise)

With the development of the game new features were added to the game. It stated with the multiplayer mode. Were players were able to play online and connect with their friends or complete strangers and compete with one another in different modes.
